The Evolution of Web Browsers

Introduction

Web browsers have evolved tremendously since the early 1990s, changing how we access and interact with the internet.

Key Browsers Through History

  • Mosaic – The first widely-used graphical browser.
  • Internet Explorer – Dominated the market for many years.
  • Firefox – Open-source and privacy-focused alternative.
  • Chrome – Currently the most popular browser worldwide.
